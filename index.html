Welcome to the MEB website

We are a group of people interested in mathematics publishing our own essay collection for a wide variety of readers. In our first issue you can read about mathematical games you can play with your friends, bio-statistical data analysis, formalization using Lean and practical experiments to estimate π. You can read these essays here.

If you are a math person from Bonn and would like to help with the next edition of MEB, you can write us an e-mail at infomeb@uni-bonn.de. If you want to be added to our WhatsApp community, include your phone number! To get started with writing your essay, clone our repository https://github.com/MEB-Collective/MEB..
